## Tiers

| Phase | Target uptime | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| **Pilot (day 0-30)** | 99.5% with tariffed compensation | External prober against `/partner/v1/health` |
| **Post-pilot (day 31+)** | 99.9% contractual | Same |
| **Enterprise tier** | 99.9% from day 1 | Dedicated multi-region replication |

## Probe contract

- Prober: Pingdom, UptimeRobot or StatusCake.
- Frequency: 1 minute interval, 3+ geographic locations (EU + US + Asia minimum).
- Up = HTTP 200 with body `{"status":"ok"}`.
- Probe IPs go on our allowlist (no rate limit). Submit them at
  `partners@privataswap.com`.
- Network failure on the probe side (provable via 3rd-party check) is excluded.

## Pilot compensation

| Uptime | Action |
|---|---|
| ≥99.5% | No compensation. |
| 99.0-99.5% (3.6-7.2 h downtime/mo) | +10% rev-share uplift next calendar month. |
| 95.0-99.0% (7.2-36 h) | +25% rev-share uplift + right to terminate pilot without notice. |
| &lt;95.0% | +100% rev-share current month + termination. |

For partners with &lt;100 orders/mo, uplift converts to flat USD per pilot agreement.

## Post-pilot SLA credits

| Uptime in month | Credit |
|---|---|
| &lt;99.9% | 10% of gross partner revenue that month |
| &lt;99.0% | 25% |
| &lt;95.0% | 100% |

Credit applied in the next payout cycle, no claim form needed — we calculate
from your prober data on file.

## Enterprise tier

Day-1 99.9% with:

- Dedicated Slack channel with on-call engineers
- 15-min response business hours / 1 h off-hours
- Monthly architecture review session
- 1 free RCA postmortem per month
- **$3,500/mo flat** (or $2,500/mo without review + postmortem)
- First 60 days free at confirmed ≥25K orders/mo pilot volume

Email `partners@privataswap.com` to upgrade.

## Public status

`https://status.privataswap.com` carries:

- Real-time uptime by region
- Incident history with RCA links
- Subscribe via RSS, email or Slack
- Scheduled maintenance windows (always announced ≥7 days out)
